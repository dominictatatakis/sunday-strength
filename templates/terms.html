The service

Sunday Strength sends a weekly training plan by email. Subscriptions are billed via Stripe at £5/month or £12/quarter and can be cancelled at any time from your account or the link in any email; cancellation stops future billing at the end of the current period.

Health disclaimer

Plans are general fitness information, not medical or personal training advice. Consult a doctor before starting a new exercise programme, especially if you have any medical condition or injury. You exercise at your own risk; stop any movement that causes pain.

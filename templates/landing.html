Questions

What if I miss a day?

Nothing breaks. Do the sessions you can in order — three good days beats four rushed ones. Next Sunday brings a fresh week regardless.

Can I change my days or level later?

Yes — sign in any time and change days, level, or the run day. Your next Sunday email uses the new settings.

How do I cancel?

From your account page or the link at the bottom of any email. Two clicks, no emails-begging-you-to-stay.
